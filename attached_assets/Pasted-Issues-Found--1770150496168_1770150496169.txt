Sanitization regex has subtle redundancy (Minor)                                                                                                                  
                                                                                                                                                                       
  Line 305's second pass regex:                                                                                                                                        
  /\b(\w+)([,.\-;:!?]?\s+\1){3,}\b/gi                                                                                                                                  
                                                                                                                                                                       
  The ? makes punctuation optional, so after the first pass already handles space-only patterns, the second pass might partially re-match. Not a bug, but could be     
  simplified to require punctuation: [,.\-;:!?]\s+ instead of [,.\-;:!?]?\s+.

Worth confirming the intended behavior.
