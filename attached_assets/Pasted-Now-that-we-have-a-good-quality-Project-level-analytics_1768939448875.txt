● This exploration reveals a clear opportunity. Currently, the Project only captures research context (objective, audience), but not strategic business     
  context - the "why" behind the research that would shape actionable recommendations.                                                                      
                                                                                                                                                            
  Recommended Feature: Strategic Context                                                                                                                    
                                                                                                                                                            
  What to Capture                                                                                                                                           
                                                                                                                                                            
  I'd recommend adding a Strategic Context section to Project setup with these fields:                                                                      
  Field: strategicGoal                                                                                                                                      
  Purpose: The business outcome driving this research                                                                                                       
  Example: "Launch a monthly newsletter that drives repeat purchases"                                                                                       
  ────────────────────────────────────────                                                                                                                  
  Field: businessContext                                                                                                                                    
  Purpose: Domain/industry context and constraints                                                                                                          
  Example: "Specialist online photography store, 5k email subscribers, competing with large retailers on expertise not price"                               
  ────────────────────────────────────────                                                                                                                  
  Field: decisionToMake                                                                                                                                     
  Purpose: What decision will these insights inform?                                                                                                        
  Example: "What topics to cover in first 3 newsletter editions"                                                                                            
  ────────────────────────────────────────                                                                                                                  
  Field: successMetrics                                                                                                                                     
  Purpose: How will you measure success?                                                                                                                    
  Example: "30% open rate, 5% click-through to product pages"                                                                                               
  How It Would Flow                                                                                                                                         
                                                                                                                                                            
  Project Setup (new fields)                                                                                                                                
    └─ strategicGoal, businessContext, decisionToMake, successMetrics                                                                                       
        │                                                                                                                                                   
        ▼                                                                                                                                                   
  Project Analytics AI Prompt (enhanced)                                                                                                                    
    └─ "Given the strategic goal of [launching a newsletter],                                                                                               
        and business context of [specialist photography store],                                                                                             
        which themes are most actionable for [deciding newsletter topics]?"                                                                                 
        │                                                                                                                                                   
        ▼                                                                                                                                                   
  Tailored Output                                                                                                                                           
    └─ Recommendations framed as newsletter topic suggestions                                                                                               
    └─ Themes ranked by relevance to repeat purchases                                                                                                       
    └─ Verbatims selected for newsletter-ready quotes
